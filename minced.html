Here are the ingredients

  • 1 kg of minced pork or beef
  • 5 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of Hoisin sauce

Here are the steps

  1. Put oil in the pan, heat it up
  2. Put the meat in, mix until half brown half red
  3. Add the sauces in any order
  4. Mix them up until cooked
  5. Eat
